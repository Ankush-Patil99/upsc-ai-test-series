          UPSC AI TEST SERIES: FUTURE ROADMAP & MONETIZATION

PART 1: FRIENDS SECTION & ENGAGEMENT FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------
*  1. Friend Request System: A "Pending Requests" inbox to accept/decline invites.
*  2. "View Stats" Comparative Modal: A side-by-side radar chart overlay comparing your subject accuracy and streaks with a friend.
*  3. Cheer & Nudge Mechanics: Send a quick "Cheer 👏" to friends performing well, or a "Nudge 🔔" to friends who lost their streak.
*  4. "New Challenge" Creation Flow: A modal to select a friend, pick a subject (e.g., Polity), and wager Tier Points (e.g., Bet 50 TP). Winner takes the pot.
*  5. Challenge History & Win Rate: A sub-tab showing past challenge outcomes and head-to-head win rates against specific friends.
*  6. Live Activity Ticker: A small scrolling feed in the network tab (e.g., "Rahul just hit a 10-day streak!").
*  7. Dynamic Leaderboard Sorting & Trends: Sort the leaderboard by "Accuracy", "Streak", or "Points". Show rank progressions (🟢 +2, 🔴 -1).
*  8. Tier/League Show-off: Display the user's current Analytics Tier (e.g., Scholar, Elite) next to their name on the leaderboard.
*  9. Co-op Milestones: If a "Paired Streak" hits 7 or 30 days, both users get special co-op badges and a confetti animation.

PART 2: PREMIUM FEATURES (MONETIZATION STRATEGY)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
✅ 1. Unlimited AI Question Generation: Free tier gets 20-30 MCQs/day. Premium gets unlimited RAG-based generation.
   2. Advanced AI Analytics & Predictor: Free tier sees basic accuracy. Premium unlocks deep topic weakness maps, time-taken analysis, and an "Expected Prelims Score" predictor.
✅ 3. Automated Remedial Tests: One-click "Mistakes Test". The AI automatically builds a custom test using only questions the user got wrong or tagged as 'Doubt' in the past.
   4. Current Affairs Deep Dives: Premium access to an auto-updated database of summarized Hindu/Indian Express editorials with connected MCQs.
   5. 1-on-1 AI Mentor Chatbot: An AI assistant that knows the user's entire attempt history and can be asked things like: "Why am I failing Economics?" or "Explain Repo Rate based on the question I got wrong yesterday."
✅ 6. "Upload Your Own Notes" Feature: Premium users can upload their coaching PDFs (Vision IAS, etc.) and have the AI generate MCQs directly from their personal files.
✅ 7. Profile Flexing (Cosmetics): Animated profile borders, a shiny "Gold/Premium" badge on the global leaderboard, and custom avatars.
✅ 8. PDF Exports: Ability to download AI-generated tests, detailed solutions, and personalized study plans as clean PDFs for offline reading.

PART 3: GROWTH, RETENTION, & PLATFORM ENHANCEMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1. All-India Live Scheduled Mocks: Host a massive "Sunday Prelims Showdown" at a specific time (e.g., 9:30 AM). Users compete live, and ranks are published immediately after. Drives massive weekend traffic.
2. Daily Current Affairs Micro-Quizzes: A short, daily 5-question quiz. Extremely effective for retaining Daily Active Users (DAUs) and building habits.
3. Study Streak Freezes: Borrowing from Duolingo, allow users to "buy" a streak freeze using their earned Tier Points (TP) so they don't lose a 50-day streak if they take a day off.
4. Gamified Onboarding Tour: When a new user signs up, give them an interactive tour of the dashboard and award them an "Initiation Badge" immediately to hook them.
5. Community Flashcards/Notes: Allow users to create flashcards and share them with their friends or the global community.
6. Topic Mastery Skill Trees: Instead of just bar charts, visualize the UPSC syllabus as a gaming "Skill Tree". Users unlock branches (e.g., "Modern History" -> "Gandhian Era") as they score above 80% in those topics.
7. Push Notifications/Telegram Integration: Optional opt-in for a daily reminder bot on Telegram/WhatsApp to do their revision.

PART 4: PAYMENT INFRASTRUCTURE & UI SECURITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Payment Gateway Integration: Implementing robust APIs (e.g., Razorpay for India, Stripe for International) to handle recurring subscriptions securely.
2. Secure Webhooks: Building backend listeners to instantly process payment successes/failures and update the user's Premium status in real-time to avoid service interruption.
3. Seamless Checkout UI: Designing an in-app checkout experience that feels native and trustworthy, with clear terms, transparent cancellation policies, and no hidden fees to build user trust.
4. Anti-Abuse Mechanisms: Preventing account sharing (concurrent login limits) and rate-limiting API endpoints to ensure free tier users cannot bypass daily question generation limits.
5. Invoicing & Compliance: Automated generation of GST-compliant invoices sent to user emails upon successful subscription or renewal.
